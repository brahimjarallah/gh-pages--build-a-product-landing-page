WatchBox

Buy, Sell & Trade Luxury Watches!

WatchBox application offers you extra functionality. It is really the cutting-edge mobile app for watch enthusiasts, offering secondary market pricing analysis, watch education, collection exploration and the latest news. Add your watches to my WatchBox to track the value of your collection, and start a Wishlist to see when those grails are within reach!

Features

News

Stay up to date on the latest watch news from a selection of leading watch blogs + enthusiast magazines. Subscribe to alerts from your favorite publications. Search for specific brand or model citations.

Store

Store pictures of your watches and maintain insurance, warranty, service history and more. Schedule repairs or routine service, request appraisals and sell pieces from your personal watch collection to our estate department in minutes.

Sell

Get a fast, free quote to sell your watch for cash, or trade it in for a new watch. Take a picture, fill out a few forms and you are on your way!
